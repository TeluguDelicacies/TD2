Our Product Categories

Ready-to-Eat

Ready-to-Eat తినడానికి సిద్ధం

Convenient and delicious ready-to-eat items that save time without compromising on authentic taste. Perfect for busy lifestyles.

Ready-to-Eat Parota

Soft, flaky, and authentic Parota that's ready to eat in minutes. Made with traditional recipes and premium ingredients.

  • No preservatives or artificial flavors
  • Traditional South Indian taste
  • Convenient packaging for freshness
  • Ready in just 2-3 minutes

Ready-to-Eat Chapathi

Wholesome and nutritious Chapathi made from quality wheat flour. Perfect for healthy meals anytime.

  • Made from premium wheat flour
  • Soft and fresh texture
  • No artificial additives
  • Ideal for daily consumption
Ready-to-Cook

Ready-to-Cook వండటానికి సిద్ధం

Semi-prepared items that require minimal cooking. Enjoy homemade taste with reduced preparation time.

Frozen Parota

Premium frozen Parota that cooks to perfection in minutes. Maintains the authentic taste and texture of freshly made Parota.

  • Flash-frozen to preserve freshness

  • No thawing required
  • Restaurant-quality taste at home

  • 100% whole wheat flour
  • No artificial preservatives

Telugu Podis

Ready-to-Use Podis తెలుగు పొడులు

Traditional Telugu spice powders that instantly enhance your meals. Made with authentic recipes passed down through generations.

Gongura Podi (గోంగూర పొడి)

Tangy and flavorful podi made from fresh Gongura leaves, a signature taste of Telugu cuisine.

  • Made from fresh Gongura leaves
  • Traditional Andhra recipe
  • Perfect with rice and ghee
  • Rich in vitamins and antioxidants

Pesalu Podi (పెసలు పొడి)

Protein-rich podi made from green gram (pesalu), offering a nutty flavor and nutritional benefits.

  • High in plant-based protein
  • Roasted to perfection
  • Naturally gluten-free
  • Great for weight management

Nuvvula Podi (నువ్వుల పొడి)

Aromatic sesame seed powder that adds a rich, nutty flavor to your meals.

  • Made from premium sesame seeds
  • Rich in healthy fats and minerals
  • Enhances flavor of any dish
  • Traditional preparation method

Kobbari Podi (కొబ్బరి పొడి)

Sweet and fragrant coconut-based podi that's perfect for rice, dosas, and idlis.

  • Fresh coconut and jaggery
  • Natural sweetness
  • Perfect for breakfast items
  • No refined sugar added
