Kadapa Kaaram

A fiery and flavorful spice blend from the Kadapa region, known for its perfect balance of heat and taste. Made with carefully selected red chilies and traditional spices.

  • Authentic Kadapa region recipe
  • Perfect balance of heat and flavor
  • Made with premium red chilies
  • Ready to eat with rice and ghee

Nalla Kaaram

Premium black pepper powder blend that adds warmth and depth to any dish. Rich in antioxidants and known for its digestive properties.

  • Premium black pepper blend
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Aids digestion
  • Perfect with idli and dosa

Traditional lentil-based spice powder that's protein-rich and flavorful. Perfect for mixing with rice and ghee for a nutritious meal.

  • High protein lentil base
  • Traditional Telugu recipe
  • Nutritious and filling
  • Great with hot rice

  • Aromatic spice blend
  • High fiber content
  • Crunchy texture

  • Authentic aroma
  • Rich in vitamins
  • Traditional preparation

Fresh, Ready-to-Cook Malabar Parota and Wheat Chapathi – Expertly prepared for your convenience. Simply heat on a pan to enjoy soft, flavorful flatbreads that complement any meal with authentic taste and texture.

Malabar Parota

Authentic Kerala-style layered parota made with premium flour and traditional techniques. Soft, flaky layers that separate beautifully when cooked. Perfect with curry or dal.

  • Authentic Kerala-style preparation
  • Multiple flaky layers
  • Made with premium flour
  • Ready in 3-4 minutes

Wheat Chapathi

Soft, wholesome wheat chapathi made from carefully selected wheat flour. Perfect balance of nutrition and taste, ideal for wrapping curries or enjoying with dal and vegetables.

  • 100% wheat flour
  • Soft and flexible texture
  • High in fiber and nutrients
  • Perfect for healthy meals

Pure Ghee

  • High in fiber and protein
  • Perfect for healthy rotis
  • Enhanced nutritional value

  • Premium coffee bean blend
